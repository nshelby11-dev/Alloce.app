1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Allocé Technologies LLC (“Allocé,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Allocé mobile application and related services (the “Service”). By using Allocé, you agree to the data practices described in this policy.

Our core principle: your financial data belongs to you. We collect only what is necessary to provide the Service and we never sell your personal information.

2. Who Can Use Allocé

Allocé is strictly for users 16 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect, use, or store personal information from anyone under 16. If we become aware a user is under 16, we will immediately suspend the account and delete all associated data. Parents or guardians who believe their child under 16 has registered should contact us at legal@alloce.app immediately.

Note on COPPA

The Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) restricts online collection of personal data from children under 13. Because our minimum age is 16 — which exceeds COPPA’s threshold — we do not collect data from anyone under 13 and have no mechanism to do so. Our age gate goes above and beyond COPPA’s requirements.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Provide

  • Account credentials: email address and password used to create your account
  • Financial data: income amounts, bill names and amounts, savings goals, spending categories, and budget splits you enter manually
  • Uploaded documents: bank statement PDFs, images, or CSV files you choose to import for transaction parsing
  • Support communications: emails or messages you send us
We never ask for your bank credentials, Social Security Number, or government-issued ID. All financial data you enter is data you type or upload yourself.

3.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • App usage data: which screens you visit and features you use — used to improve the product
  • Device and OS information: device type, operating system version, app version, for compatibility and debugging
  • Crash reports: anonymized error logs when the app crashes, to identify and fix bugs
  • Log data: IP address (approximate), timestamps, and error logs for security monitoring

3.3 Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not access your bank accounts, credit cards, or financial institutions directly
  • We do not collect biometric data
  • We do not collect precise real-time location data
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites
  • We do not collect Social Security Numbers or government IDs

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Service, including syncing your budget data across devices
  • To authenticate your identity and secure your account
  • To send push notifications you have enabled (bill due dates, payday reminders)
  • To respond to customer support requests
  • To improve and develop new features based on aggregated, anonymized usage patterns
  • To detect, prevent, and respond to fraud, abuse, or unauthorized access
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations
We do not use your financial data for advertising. We do not share your data with advertising networks. Allocé is ad-free and we do not sell your data.

5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information or financial data to third parties. We may share your information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers — Trusted vendors who help us operate the Service (cloud hosting, analytics, crash reporting, email delivery), bound by contractual data processing agreements

  • Business Transfers — In connection with a merger or acquisition; we will notify you before your data transfers to a different privacy policy
  • With Your Consent — In any other circumstance with your explicit prior consent

6. Data Storage & Security

6.1 Where Your Data Lives

Your account and budget data is stored securely in Supabase, our cloud database provider. Supabase is hosted on AWS and maintains SOC 2 Type II compliance. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS/HTTPS) and at rest.

Row Level Security (RLS) is enforced on all database tables. Your data is technically isolated at the database level — queries are scoped to your user ID only.

6.2 Data Retention

  • Active accounts: data retained for the life of your account
  • Deleted accounts: all personal data purged within 90 days of account deletion
  • Uploaded documents (bank statements, etc.): deleted within 30 days of account closure
  • Anonymized usage analytics: may be retained indefinitely
  • Support communications: retained for up to 3 years for recordkeeping

6.3 Security Practices

  • All API keys and secrets are stored in environment variables, never in the app codebase
  • Authentication uses PKCE flow, the most secure method available for mobile apps
  • We conduct regular security reviews of our Supabase RLS policies
